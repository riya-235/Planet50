What is Sustainability?

Sustainability means making decisions that are good for people, the planet, and future generations. It includes protecting natural resources, ensuring fairness, and building a strong economy. Click below to explore the 3 main types of sustainability.

Environmental Sustainability

This focuses on protecting Earth’s ecosystems and natural resources. It involves reducing carbon emissions, using renewable energy, recycling, and conserving wildlife and forests.

Social Sustainability

This is about creating a fair and inclusive society. It means ensuring everyone has access to clean water, healthcare, education, and housing, while promoting equity and justice.

Economic Sustainability

This ensures long-term economic health by supporting responsible growth. It includes investing in clean technologies, creating green jobs, and building resilient local economies.
