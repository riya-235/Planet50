Planet50 is a text-based strategy game where you lead a city through the climate crisis. Your goal is to make sustainable choices that help your community survive and thrive until the year 2075.

Objective

Make smart decisions to increase your sustainability score and maintain strong public approval. If your scores are too low, your city will face collapse.

How It Works

  • The game starts in the year 2025.
  • Each round, you'll face a scenario with 4 choices (A‚ÄìD).
  • Your decisions affect your Sustainability Score and Public Approval.
  • Random events like weather disasters, crises, and public votes can also change your scores.
  • Policy cards and global treaties may appear and give you bonus effects.
  • The game ends in the year 2075 with a final score report and achievements.

Tips for Playing

  • Think long term, some choices look easy now but hurt later.
  • Balance the environment, economy, and public happiness.
  • Use policy cards wisely when you're falling behind.
  • Watch for crisis and weather impacts each round.

Win Conditions

  • Sustainability Score of 60 or more
  • Public Approval of 60 or more
